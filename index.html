Build Your Own Sub at Firehouse Subs: Customize Your Culinary Adventure

Firehouse Subs is a sandwich lover's paradise, offering a wide range of delicious subs, hearty portions, and fresh ingredients. Known for its robust flavors and premium ingredients, Firehouse Subs also gives you the opportunity to build your own sub — allowing you to create a custom sandwich tailored exactly to your tastes. Whether you prefer a classic combination or want to push the envelope with unique flavor profiles, Firehouse Subs makes it easy to design a sandwich that's uniquely yours.

In this article, we will guide you through the exciting process of building your own sub, including Firehouse Subs' menu options, popular combinations, and prices, so you can make the most out of your culinary adventure.

Step 1: Choose Your Bread – The Foundation of Your Sub

Your sub starts with the bread, and Firehouse Subs offers several freshly baked bread options to suit any palate. Each one provides a perfect canvas for your delicious creation.

Bread Options at Firehouse Subs:

  • Classic White Sub Roll: Soft, slightly chewy, and perfect for any combination of fillings. ($5.99 for a small, $7.99 for a large)
  • Whole Wheat Sub Roll: A healthier option with a mild nutty flavor. ($5.99 for a small, $7.99 for a large)
  • Hero Roll: This sub roll offers an extra layer of crunch while still being soft inside. ($6.49 for a small, $8.49 for a large)
  • Gluten-Free Bread: Ideal for those with dietary restrictions, offering the same great taste without the gluten. ($6.49 for a small, $8.49 for a large)

Step 3: Add Toppings – The Flavor Explosion

Firehouse Subs allows you to take your sub to the next level with a wide range of fresh toppings. Whether you want something simple and fresh or something more flavorful and indulgent, the choice is yours.

Vegetables & Fresh Toppings:

  • Lettuce: Crisp and fresh, a classic choice for any sub.
  • Tomatoes: Juicy, ripe tomatoes for a burst of flavor.
  • Onions: Choose from raw red onions or caramelized onions for extra flavor.
  • Pickles: Tangy and crunchy—great for adding zest to any sandwich.
  • Banana Peppers: Mildly tangy and slightly spicy, a great addition for those who like a little kick.

Cheeses:

  • Provolone: A mild, smooth cheese that melts wonderfully.
  • Cheddar: Sharp, rich, and full of flavor.
  • Monterey Jack: Creamy, mild, and slightly tangy.
  • Pepper Jack: For those who enjoy a bit of spice, this cheese has a nice kick.

Step 4: Sauces & Condiments – Craft Your Perfect Flavor Profile

What’s a sub without the perfect sauce? Firehouse Subs offers a variety of sauces to complement your sandwich, ranging from zesty and tangy to smoky and spicy.

Popular Sauces at Firehouse Subs:

  • Mustard: Classic yellow mustard or spicy brown mustard for a tangy flavor.
  • Mayo: Creamy and rich for a smooth texture.
  • BBQ Sauce: Sweet Baby Ray's BBQ sauce adds a smoky, sweet kick—ideal for pulled pork or brisket.
  • Zesty Italian Dressing: Light and tangy, perfect for Italian-style subs.
  • Sriracha: A fiery, bold sauce for those who like things hot.
  • Firehouse Subs’ Hot Sauce: Take it up a notch with a range of hot sauces available at the self-serve hot sauce bar. Choose from mild to wild!

Condiments to Add Extra Flavor:

  • Olive Oil & Vinegar: Adds a fresh and tangy finish to any sub.
  • Salt & Pepper: Simple seasonings that enhance the flavors of your sub.

Step 5: Toast It – The Perfect Finish

While you can enjoy your sub cold, many Firehouse Subs fans prefer to have their creations toasted. Toasting adds a delicious crunch to the bread, while melting the cheese and blending the flavors. It’s the perfect finishing touch to a freshly made sub.

Step 6: Pair with a Drink & Side – The Full Experience

No sub is complete without the right drink and sides. Firehouse Subs offers a selection of beverages, from sodas to iced tea and lemonade. You can also add a side to your meal for a more fulfilling dining experience.

Drink Options:

  • Fountain sodas (Coke, Sprite, Dr. Pepper, etc.)
  • Iced tea and lemonade
  • Bottled water

Side Options:

  • Chips: Choose from a variety of chips like Lays, Doritos, or Miss Vickie’s.
  • Firehouse Chili: A rich, hearty chili made with beef, beans, and savory spices.
  • Side Salad: Fresh and crisp, a healthy option to balance your sub.
  • Cookie: For dessert, enjoy a sweet treat like a chocolate chip cookie.
